What is Batch Screenshot Cropping?

CropStack is a free browser-based batch screenshot cropper that allows you to crop hundreds of images instantly using pixel values. It is useful for social media managers, researchers, designers, and marketers who work with large numbers of screenshots.

Instead of cropping images one by one, you can upload all screenshots, enter crop values once, and CropStack will process them in bulk. All processing happens locally in your browser, meaning your images are never uploaded to any server.
